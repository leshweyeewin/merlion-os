# 📊 Live Data Dashboard & Exact Data Sources

All data panels in the **SG Hub Dashboard** load on-demand when clicked and show a **"Last synced"** SGT timestamp. Below are the exact sources and APIs feeding the UI:

| UI Sub-Panel | Data Source / API Endpoint | Display Details |
|---|---|---|
| 🌤️ **Weather & Air Quality** | **NEA API** (`https://api-open.data.gov.sg/v2/real-time/api/`: `psi`, `pm25`, `two-hr-forecast`, `twenty-four-hr-forecast`, `air-temperature`, `relative-humidity`, `wind-speed`, `wind-direction`, `rainfall`, `uv-index`) | Visual PSI gauge + 6-region 2-hour forecast cards, PM2.5, live "Current Conditions" tiles (temperature, humidity, wind speed/direction via circular mean, rainfall, UV Index with NEA 5-tier scale), and a 24-hour general outlook. |
| 🚇 **Transit & Rail Alerts** | **LTA DataMall API** (`TrainServiceAlerts`) + **ICA Newsroom Dynamic Endpoint** (`https://www.ica.gov.sg/ICAContentInterface/MediaReleasesList/FindMediaReleases`) | Live line-by-line status grid (EWL, NSL, NEL, CCL, DTL, TEL, LRTs) with disruption logs, free public bus boarding notices, and free MRT shuttle routes. Also includes a dedicated **ICA Checkpoint & News Updates** panel listing real-time border checkpoint advisories (e.g. Woodlands/Tuas delays) and media releases. |
| 🚕 **Transport & Vehicle Costs** | **LTA DataMall API** (`Taxi-Availability`) + **data.gov.sg Dataset API** (COE Bidding Results / Prices, `d_69b3380ad7e51aff3a7dcc84eba52b8a`) | Live islandwide taxi count and the latest COE bidding premiums for all 5 vehicle categories (A–E), each with a **demand-momentum read** — round-over-round premium change plus a bids-to-quota oversubscription ratio (e.g. "1.51x bids/quota — fierce bidding"), so a category can't look flat purely off one round's premium while bidding pressure is quietly building underneath. A rule-based **"why" explanation** compares quota and bid-volume changes round-over-round to say whether a premium move was a supply story, a demand story, or both (shown only when either moved ≥5%). An "Around You (2km)" button (browser geolocation, requested only on click) shows a live nearby count within 2km plus the nearest planning area name, reverse-geocoded against a built-in list of Singapore's major towns. |
| 📢 **Gov Updates** | **Telegram Scraper** (12 Channels: `@govsg`, `@HealthHubSG`, `@scamshieldalert`, `@LTAsg`, `@NEAsg`, `@MOEsg`, `@GovTechSG`, `@MOHSingapore`, `@SPFsg`, `@SCDFsg`, `@momsg`, `@ReachSingapore`) + **PUB Flood Alerts API** (`https://api-open.data.gov.sg/v2/real-time/api/weather/flood-alerts`) | Last 3 posts per channel sorted chronologically descending by SGT post date; active flood alerts shown as a priority banner above the feed. |
| 🏢 **HDB BTO Tracker** | **HDB Pulse & Newsroom Scraper** (`https://www.hdb.gov.sg/hdb-pulse/news`) + **Enhanced CPF Housing Grant (EHG) Estimator** | Live BTO launch tables + BeautifulSoup HDB newsroom Next.js `__NEXT_DATA__` JSON extraction (resolving real CMS URLs dynamically), combined with an instant grant estimator based on household income. |
| 🏷️ **HDB Resale Flat Prices** | **data.gov.sg Dataset API** (Resale Flat Prices, `d_8b84c4ee58e3cfc0ece0d773c8ca6abc`) + **CPF HDB Accrued Interest Calculator** | Real islandwide median resale price, YoY trend, ranked town breakdown for the latest complete month, and an interactive calculator to estimate compounding accrued interest (2.5% annually) to be returned to CPF OA upon property sale. A rule-based **"why" explanation** compares each flat type's own YoY median change against the headline islandwide figure to flag when the reported change is broad-based across flat types versus largely a mix-shift (e.g. more executive flats transacting, not genuine price movement). |
| 📊 **Job Market Analysis** | **Google BigQuery** (real MOM Job Vacancy by Industry & Occupation data, loaded via `scripts/load_job_vacancy_to_bigquery.py`), with automatic fallback to a direct **data.gov.sg Dataset API** call if BigQuery isn't configured; cross-referenced against the **MOM Retrenched Employees by Industry** dataset (`d_61d92d31ca400be135190614277da825`) for the Hiring Pressure Index below | Real vacancy counts, YoY trend, and a next-year forecast per sector (Tech, Finance, Healthcare, General), plus an interactive **multi-year vacancy trend line chart** (2014→latest, hover crosshair with exact counts per sector). Also computes a **Hiring Pressure Index** — same-year vacancies ÷ retrenchments in the same industries — so a sector can't look healthy purely off a positive YoY delta while it's shedding workers just as fast; classified strong/moderate/balanced/weak, or flagged as pure hiring growth when zero retrenchments are recorded. And a **multi-year CAGR trend-break check** — compares this year's YoY change against the sector's own CAGR over the fetched window (up to 6 years) and flags when the two diverge by ≥3pp, so a positive YoY figure sitting on top of a much steeper (or shallower) multi-year trend reads as deceleration/acceleration rather than a flat percentage. A rule-based **"why" explanation** cross-references the trend-break verdict against the Hiring Pressure Index — e.g. distinguishing "accelerating because of genuine net hiring demand" from "accelerating but mostly churn, since retrenchments are rising just as fast" — shown only when both readings are available and the divergence is meaningful. All displayed figures are real; the former illustrative median-salary/skills/risk fields were removed. |
| ⚠️ **MOM Retrenchment** | **data.gov.sg Dataset API** (MOM Retrenched Employees by Industry, Quarterly, `d_61d92d31ca400be135190614277da825`) | Real latest-quarter retrenchment headcount and top affected industries, plus a **24-quarter retrenchment trend chart** with an auto-computed "vs recent average" takeaway. (The former illustrative six-month re-employment rate was removed.) |
| 💼 **Occupational Wage Explorer** | **MOM Occupational Wage Survey Excel tables** (stats.mom.gov.sg "Occupational Wages Tables" page, table1 T1 sheet, two most recent June editions) | Insights-first breakdown of 500+ detailed job titles: key takeaways summary, a **wage-distribution histogram** (how many titles per salary band, with the cross-title median), a **pay-vs-raise scatter plot** (every occupation plotted by wage level × YoY change, tech/digital roles highlighted, per-dot hover tooltips), top 5 wage risers/decliners, top 5 highest-paying tech & digital roles, notable genuinely new (SSOC 2024 / AI-era) job titles (renamed titles fuzzy-matched to their old rows), and a search-driven wage lookup that defaults to a top-10 leaderboard. A rule-based **"why" explanation** compares tech/AI roles' average YoY wage change against the rest of the workforce to flag whether the AI-era wage story is real pay growth or mostly just new job creation (shown only when the gap is ≥2pp with sufficient title coverage on both sides). (The former 25th/75th percentile enrichment from the one-off June 2024 data.gov.sg edition was removed under the same freshness policy.) |
| 🎟️ **Kiasu SG Deals** | **Telegram Scraper** (15 Channels: `@confirmgood`, `@goodlobang`, `@kiasufoodies`, `@sgweekend`, `@moneydigest`, etc.) | Community deals and lifestyle news posted strictly within the last 24 hours, sorted newest-first. |
| ⚖️ **IRAS Tax & Wealth** | **IRAS Due Dates Scraping Endpoint** (`https://www.iras.gov.sg/due-dates`) + **Personal Tax Relief Optimizer** (progressive resident brackets YA 2024–2026) | Official chronological filing calendar deadlines (Individual, Corporate, GST) scraped live from IRAS, plus an interactive CPF Special Account (RSTU) vs. SRS Top-up Optimizer with dollar-saved calculator and asset liquidity trade-off advisories. See [iras_optimizer.md](iras_optimizer.md). |
